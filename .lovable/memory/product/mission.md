# Mission

Dynamo's mission is to **scale face-to-face discourse to a 21st-century standard** by providing a revolutionarily convenient means to organize conversations, record them, and build from them.

We believe that **words matter, but only when there are people who can own up to them** and be held responsible for them and the decisions they create or contribute to.

We believe the **destiny of humanity is best left to the helm of our collective intellect**, not the whim of privatized interests.

We foster an environment and community that can sustain the political climate of a world on the shoulders of imperfect people.

**Persuasion is power. People can be persuasive, especially if they can practice.**

Our mission is to **bring people to the power.**

## How to apply
Every product decision must serve at least one of: (1) making organizing a conversation easier, (2) making the record more durable and accountable, (3) helping people practice persuasion. Reject features that obscure authorship, dilute responsibility, or hand discourse back to privatized algorithmic feeds.
